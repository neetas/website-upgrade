ABOUT

I am Neeta, a freelance musician, music composer/producer, sound designer & technologist based in the UK. I am passionate about electronic music, and particularly interested in the boundary that lies between live/acoustic performance and electronic sound. I love many aspects of the music making process and over the years have developed a diverse range of skills both in the studio and on the stage. My sound as a composer ranges in style covering soul, electronica, cinematic, ambient, orchestral, Indian fusion, world, classical, RnB, hip-hop, jazz, vocal, acoustic, folk, pop, drum and bass & house.

I have designed loop packs for a number of sample pack companies including Samplephonics, FreakyLoops, Noiiz, and currently create ongoing bespoke sound packs for Ampify x Novation’s apps: Launchpad, Blocs Wave & Groovebox for iOS. I have composed music to brief for film, tv and podcasts. Recent work includes theme tunes for BBC. I have collaborated with AI music engines, arranging and producing music written by them as well as composing music for them.

I am a classically trained pianist, starting age 7. As a teenager I found a great passion for songwriting and singing, and soon after began experimenting with recording/production using Cubase. I graduated in 2008 from Leeds College of Music where I studied Popular Music (Major Composition - 1st). At Uni I played in a number of bands, realising my compositions live. My sound as an artist fuses electronic, soulful, downtempo and sometimes Indian flavours. As an artist I’ve released music on independent labels and performed around the UK in bands. Many of my original tracks have received national radio play and have been used for sync.

I am also an ex-software developer and I still feel very passionate about technology. I love experimental ideas using music and new technology, and welcome any projects that marry the two.

Please enjoy my showreel and email any enquiries to neetamusic@gmail.com.
